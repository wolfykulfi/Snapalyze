About Snapalyze

Snapalyze is an AI-powered image analysis tool that uses Google's Gemini API to analyze and describe images.

Simply upload an image, provide instructions for analysis, and get detailed AI-generated insights about your image.

This tool respects your privacy - images are processed securely and not stored permanently.
